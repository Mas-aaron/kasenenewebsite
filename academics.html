Our Curriculum

Primary Education (P1-P7)

Our primary curriculum provides a strong foundation in core subjects while nurturing creativity and critical thinking skills.

Core Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education

Our core subjects form the foundation of primary education, with emphasis on literacy, numeracy, and scientific thinking.

Languages

  • Luganda (Local Language)
  • French (Introductory)
  • Swahili (Introductory)

We emphasize multilingual education to prepare students for our diverse society and global opportunities.

Creative Arts

  • Art and Craft
  • Music
  • Drama
  • Physical Education

Creative expression is nurtured through various artistic and physical activities that develop confidence and teamwork.

Ordinary Level (S1-S4)

Our O-Level program prepares students for both academic advancement and practical life skills.

Sciences

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology
  • Mathematics

Our science program includes practical laboratory work and projects to develop scientific inquiry skills.

Humanities

  • History
  • Geography
  • Religious Education
  • Literature in English

Humanities subjects help students understand society, culture, and human behavior in historical context.

Vocational

  • Agriculture
  • Commerce
  • Computer Studies
  • Home Economics

Vocational subjects provide practical skills that can lead to entrepreneurship or further specialization.

Advanced Level (S5-S6)

Our A-Level program offers specialization in three main combinations to prepare students for university education.

Science Combination

  • Mathematics
  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Biology or ICT

Prepares students for careers in medicine, engineering, technology, and pure sciences.

Arts Combination

  • History
  • Geography
  • Economics
  • Literature or Divinity

Ideal for students pursuing careers in law, education, social sciences, and arts.

Business Combination

  • Economics
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Mathematics
  • ICT or Geography

Designed for future business leaders, accountants, and management professionals.

Examinations

Our examination system is designed to assess student progress while preparing them for national examinations.

Continuous Assessment

Regular tests, quizzes, and projects account for 30% of the final grade.

Mid-Term Exams

Comprehensive exams covering material from the first half of each term.

End of Term Exams

Final examinations covering all term material, accounting for 70% of the grade.

National Exams

Preparation for PLE (Primary), UCE (O-Level), and UACE (A-Level).
