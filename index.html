Welcome to Kasenene School

Founded in 1995, Kasenene School has grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region. Our mission is to provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, spiritually, and socially.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of educators, we create an environment where students can dream big, believe in themselves, and achieve excellence with God's guidance.
