Welcome Cause of Our Joy Schools

Founded in 2021, Cause of Our Joy Schools has grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region. Our mission is to provide quality education that empowers students to achieve their full potential academically, spiritually, and socially.

With state-of-the-art facilities and a dedicated team of educators, we create an environment where students can dream big, believe in themselves, and achieve excellence with God's guidance.

From Our Founder

"I was always very interested in going to school because I wanted to become a priest or a lawyer. But school was more than 10 kilometers away. I walked this distance barefoot, and we had no proper buildings—just trees for classrooms. Despite poverty and many challenges, I was able to study up to PhD level. That's why I founded this school." — Fr. Bernard Rabwoni Akiiki

Founded in 2021, Cause of Our Joy Junior School - Kasenene began with 74 pupils in temporary wooden classrooms. Today, we serve about 280 pupils with a vision to become the leading center of holistic education in Kyegegwa District.
