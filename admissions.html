Admission Process

Our admission process is designed to be transparent and straightforward. Follow these steps to join our school community.

1

Inquiry

Contact our admissions office or visit the school to learn more about our programs and requirements.

2

Application

Complete the application form and submit required documents either online or at the school.

3

Assessment

Students may be required to take an entrance assessment depending on the class level.

4

Interview

An interview with the admissions committee may be scheduled for some applicants.

5

Acceptance

Successful applicants receive an admission letter with details for enrollment.

Admission Requirements

Primary Level (P1-P7)

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • 2 recent passport photos
  • Previous school report (if transferring)
  • Medical report form

Ordinary Level (S1-S4)

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of PLE results slip/certificate
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • 3 recent passport photos
  • Transfer form (if applicable)

Advanced Level (S5-S6)

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of UCE results slip/certificate
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • 4 recent passport photos
  • Recommendation letter from previous school

Scholarships & Financial Aid

We offer several scholarship opportunities to support talented and deserving students who may need financial assistance.

Academic Excellence Scholarship

Awarded to students who demonstrate exceptional academic performance in national or school examinations.

  • Full or partial tuition coverage
  • Minimum 1st grade in PLE or Division 1 in UCE
  • Renewable based on maintaining top performance

Sports Talent Scholarship

For students with outstanding abilities in sports who can contribute to our school teams.

  • Partial tuition coverage (25%-75%)
  • Must try out with school coaches
  • Maintain good academic standing

Need-Based Financial Aid

For students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds who show academic promise.

  • Requires comprehensive application
  • Household income verification needed
  • Community recommendation letters

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the application deadline?

We accept applications throughout the year, but for admission at the beginning of the academic year (February), we recommend submitting applications by November 30th of the previous year. Late applications may be considered if spaces are available.

Is there an entrance exam?

Entrance assessments are required for students joining Primary 4 and above. The assessment helps us understand the student's current level and determine appropriate placement. For nursery students joining Primary 1, we conduct simple readiness evaluations.

What are the school hours?

For day scholars, school runs from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM Monday to Friday, with shorter hours on Saturday (8:00 AM to 1:00 PM). Boarders follow a structured schedule that includes prep time, meals, and extracurricular activities until 9:00 PM.

Do you offer transport services?

Yes, we have school buses that serve various routes in the surrounding areas. Transport fees vary depending on distance and are paid termly. Please contact the transport office for specific route information and availability.

Can parents visit boarding students?

Parents may visit boarding students on designated visiting days, which are usually the second Saturday of each month from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Special arrangements can be made for exceptional circumstances by contacting the boarding master/mistress.
