About Kasenene School

Discover our rich history, guiding principles, and the people who make our school exceptional

Our History

1995

School Founding

Kasenene School was established with just 50 students and 5 teachers, operating from temporary structures with a vision to provide quality education to the community.

2002

First Permanent Buildings

The school constructed its first permanent classrooms and administrative block, marking the beginning of physical infrastructure development.

2010

National Recognition

Kasenene School received national recognition for academic excellence when our students ranked among the top performers in national examinations.

2018

Modern Science Complex

The state-of-the-art science laboratory complex was opened, significantly enhancing our STEM education capabilities.

2023

Current Status

Today, Kasenene School serves over 500 students with 30 qualified teachers and continues to expand its facilities and academic programs.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To provide quality holistic education that nurtures students' intellectual, spiritual, physical, and social development, grounded in Christian values, and preparing them to be responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Vision

To be a center of academic excellence that produces well-rounded individuals who positively impact their communities and the nation at large.
